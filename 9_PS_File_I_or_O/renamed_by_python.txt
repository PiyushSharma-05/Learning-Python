Every donkey on the hillside seemed to agree that the morning sun was perfect for a stroll. One donkey nudged the garden gate, while a second donkey followed close behind, hoping to find some stray hay. Soon, a third donkey joined the group, leading to a chorus of donkey brays that echoed through the valley. The farmer called out to each donkey by name, but a mischievous donkey decided to trot toward the orchard instead. Whether it was a young donkey or an old donkey, every single donkey in the herd possessed a unique personality. It was truly a donkey paradise, where a donkey could simply be a donkey.
